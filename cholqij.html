What is the Chol Q'ij?

Chol Q'ij is the Mayan calendar system, which consists of 20 day names and 13 month names. Each day name has its own unique meaning and significance, and the combination of a day name and a month name creates a specific date in the Mayan calendar.

The 20 day names are:

  • Imox
  • Ik'
  • Ak'ab'al
